Gran Canaria has many interesting and fun attraction parks, including the biggest Crocodile Park in Europe. The best known parks in Gran Canaria incude the 'Palmitos Park', the 'Cocodrilo Park' and the 'Aqualand' water park, great activities for any family or individual spending their holidays on the island.

  • Palmitos Park
  • Cocodrilo Park
  • Aqualand

Palmitos Park

The best zoological and botanical park in the Canary Islands. You can enjoy displays of Raptors, of Parrots, Exotic Birds, and besides you can admire the spectacular dolphinarium dolphins, visit the aquarium, and the largest collection of orchids in Europe.


Cocodrilo Park

Crocodile Park Gran Canaria is not only a zoo for crocodiles, it is a home to many animals brought by individuals or stolen by the government. Many of them have terrible stories of abuse and neglect to get here. By your visit, you help directly to aid us in getting more animals into veterinary care, here on the park.


Aqualand

Aqualand, a world of water attractions for the whole family to enjoy. Experience a mixture of water slides of all different shapes and sizes, from adrenaline rushes to moments of relax whilst watching the little ones enjoy themselves at their very own water playground, Children's Paradise, Aqualand has something for everyone. Enjoy water! Enjoy AQUALAND!
